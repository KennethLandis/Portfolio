About Me

I love playing strategy games and reading books. Solving a puzzle and watching a movie is one of my favorite Saturday activities.

After high school I attended two years of college and found myself lost on where to continue. Over the next 12 year I experienced different industries and workplaces attempting to find something to be passionate about. After spending roughly 400 development hours familiarizing myself with a few languages and the logic of coding, I enrolled at Bloc/Thinkful to immerse myself in a journey toward software development as my new career!
